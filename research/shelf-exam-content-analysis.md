## Exam Format
- 110 MCQs, 165 minutes
- 75-80% medical management, NOT surgical technique
- ~84-85% EPC needed for 90th percentile

## Published Evidence
- Trauma/Burns quiz scores had highest beta-coefficient (1.57, p<.001) for predicting NBME shelf performance
- Wound/ICU (1.38) and Esophagus/Anorectal (1.42) also highly predictive
- Source: PMC7391907 (156 students, 2015-2017)

## Shelf vs Step 2 CK
- Surgery = 20-30% of Step 2 CK
- Aggregate shelf correlates at r=0.60-0.75 (R²=0.36-0.56)
- Biggest gap: Shelf weights GI at 20-25% vs Step 2 CK at 5-10%
