# Surgical Critical Care Evidence Guide

Comprehensive reference for SCC topic categories, guideline societies, scoring systems, protocol frameworks, and landmark trials.

## SCC Topic Categories

### Sepsis and Infection

**Topics:** Sepsis bundles, empiric antibiotics, source control timing, septic shock resuscitation
**Key Guidelines:** Surviving Sepsis Campaign 2021, IDSA, Surgical Infection Society
**Bundle Elements:** Hour-1 bundle (lactate, cultures, antibiotics, fluids, vasopressors), Hour-3/6 re-evaluation
**Key Decision Points:** Empiric antibiotic selection, source control timing (<12h), vasopressor choice (norepi first-line), corticosteroids in refractory shock
**Landmark Trials:** ProCESS, ARISE, ProMISe (EGDT equivalence), ADRENAL (hydrocortisone in septic shock)

### Ventilator Management

**Topics:** ARDS (low tidal volume, PEEP, prone positioning), weaning protocols, tracheostomy timing
**Key Guidelines:** ARDSNet Protocol, ATS/ESICM 2017 (ARDS definition), SCCM Liberation Bundle
**Key Thresholds:** Vt 6 mL/kg IBW, Pplat <=30 cmH2O, driving pressure <=15
**Key Decision Points:** High vs low PEEP table, prone positioning criteria (P/F <150), NMB in severe ARDS, tracheostomy timing (early vs late)
**Landmark Trials:** ARMA (low Vt), PROSEVA (prone positioning), ACURASYS (NMB), ROSE (NMB replication), ART (recruitment maneuvers)

### Sedation, Analgesia, and Delirium (PADIS)

**Topics:** Sedation targets, pain assessment, delirium prevention and treatment
**Key Guidelines:** SCCM PADIS 2018, eCASH bundle
**Scoring:** RASS (target 0 to -2), CPOT (>=3 significant pain), BPS (>5 significant pain), CAM-ICU (delirium screening)
**Key Decision Points:** Analgesia-first approach, daily sedation interruption, ABCDEF bundle compliance, dexmedetomidine vs propofol, haloperidol vs atypicals for delirium
**Landmark Trials:** ABC Trial (awakening + breathing coordination), MENDS (dexmedetomidine), MIND-USA (antipsychotics for delirium), AID-ICU

### VTE Prophylaxis

**Topics:** Chemical prophylaxis timing, mechanical prophylaxis, IVC filters, trauma-specific protocols
**Key Guidelines:** EAST 2023, CHEST 2012, ASH 2018
**Scoring:** Caprini Score, trauma-specific risk factors (TBI, spinal cord injury, pelvic fracture)
**Key Decision Points:** Timing of LMWH initiation after trauma/surgery, TBI-specific protocols (24-48h delay), IVC filter indications and retrieval, TEG-guided prophylaxis
**Landmark Trials:** PREVENT Trial (IVC filters in trauma)

### Nutrition

**Topics:** Enteral vs parenteral, early feeding, protein targets, immunonutrition
**Key Guidelines:** ASPEN/SCCM 2016, ESPEN 2019
**Scoring:** NUTRIC Score (>=5 high risk)
**Key Decision Points:** Early EN (<48h), trophic vs full feeds in ARDS, protein target 1.2-2.0 g/kg/day, PN initiation timing (day 7 if EN not feasible), immunonutrition role
**Landmark Trials:** TARGET (energy-dense vs standard EN), CALORIES (early PN vs late), EPaNIC (early vs late PN)

### Transfusion and Resuscitation

**Topics:** Restrictive vs liberal, massive transfusion, TEG/ROTEM, damage control resuscitation
**Key Guidelines:** EAST, AABB, PROPPR ratios
**Key Decision Points:** Hgb threshold (7 vs 9), MTP activation (ABC score >=2), balanced ratios (1:1:1), TXA timing (<3h), calcium replacement, TEG/ROTEM-guided resuscitation
**Landmark Trials:** PROPPR (1:1:1 vs 1:1:2), CRASH-2/CRASH-3 (TXA), TRICC (restrictive transfusion), TRIBE (trauma RBC threshold)

### Renal Support

**Topics:** AKI staging, CRRT timing, dose, anticoagulation
**Key Guidelines:** KDIGO 2012, ADQI
**Scoring:** KDIGO AKI Stage 1-3 (creatinine and UOP criteria)
**Key Decision Points:** CRRT timing (early vs standard), dose (25 mL/kg/h), anticoagulation (citrate vs heparin), modality (CVVH vs CVVHDF)
**Landmark Trials:** AKIKI (early vs delayed CRRT), IDEAL-ICU, STARRT-AKI

### Stress Ulcer Prophylaxis

**Topics:** PPI vs H2RA, risk stratification, de-escalation
**Key Guidelines:** SUP-ICU 2018, PEPTIC 2020
**Risk Factors:** Mechanical ventilation >48h, coagulopathy, TBI, burns, prior GI bleed
**Key Decision Points:** PPI vs H2RA (PEPTIC showed no mortality difference), de-escalation when risk factors resolve
**Landmark Trials:** SUP-ICU (PPI vs placebo), PEPTIC (PPI vs H2RA)

### VAP Prevention

**Topics:** Oral care, HOB elevation, subglottic suctioning, antibiotic duration
**Key Guidelines:** SHEA/IDSA 2014, ATS/IDSA 2016
**Bundle Elements:** HOB 30-45 degrees, daily sedation vacation + SBT, oral care with chlorhexidine (debated), subglottic secretion drainage, DVT + peptic ulcer prophylaxis
**Key Decision Points:** Chlorhexidine oral care (benefit debated, possible harm), 7 vs 14 day antibiotics, de-escalation based on cultures

### ECMO

**Topics:** VV-ECMO indications, VA-ECMO, patient selection, complications
**Key Guidelines:** ELSO Guidelines, EOLIA criteria
**Key Decision Points:** VV for refractory ARDS (P/F <80 despite optimization), VA for cardiogenic shock, candidacy (age, reversibility, comorbidities), anticoagulation management
**Landmark Trials:** EOLIA (VV-ECMO in severe ARDS), CESAR

### Resuscitation (DCR)

**Topics:** Damage control resuscitation, permissive hypotension, balanced ratios, TXA
**Key Guidelines:** EAST, ATLS, Hartford Consensus
**Key Decision Points:** Permissive hypotension (SBP 80-90 until hemorrhage control), balanced ratio MTP (1:1:1), TXA within 3h of injury, minimize crystalloid, target-directed with TEG/ROTEM
**Landmark Trials:** PROPPR, CRASH-2, CRASH-3, PAMPer (pre-hospital plasma)

### Glucose Management

**Key Guidelines:** NICE-SUGAR, ADA/AACE
**Target Range:** 140-180 mg/dL (NICE-SUGAR showed tight control harmful)
**Landmark Trial:** NICE-SUGAR (intensive vs conventional glucose control)

### Post-Intensive Care Syndrome (PICS)

**Domains:** Physical (ICU-acquired weakness), cognitive (delirium-related impairment), psychological (PTSD, depression, anxiety)
**Key Guidelines:** SCCM THRIVE initiative
**Prevention:** ABCDEF bundle, early mobilization, ICU diary, follow-up clinic

## SCC Scoring Systems

| Score     | What It Measures         | Components                                                   | Key Thresholds           |
| --------- | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------ |
| APACHE II | ICU mortality prediction | 12 physiology vars + age + chronic health                    | Higher = worse           |
| SOFA      | Organ failure trajectory | PaO2/FiO2, platelets, bilirubin, MAP, GCS, creatinine        | >=2 increase = sepsis    |
| qSOFA     | Quick sepsis screening   | RR >=22, SBP <=100, altered mentation                        | >=2 = high risk          |
| CAM-ICU   | Delirium screening       | Acute onset, inattention, altered LOC, disorganized thinking | Positive = delirium      |
| RASS      | Sedation level           | Observer assessment                                          | -5 to +4, target 0 to -2 |
| CPOT      | Pain (non-verbal)        | Face, body, muscle tension, ventilator compliance            | 0-8, >=3 = pain          |
| BPS       | Pain (non-verbal)        | Face, upper limbs, ventilator compliance                     | 3-12, >5 = pain          |
| NUTRIC    | Nutrition risk           | Age, APACHE II, SOFA, comorbidities                          | >=5 = high risk          |
| Caprini   | VTE risk                 | Surgery, age, BMI, comorbidities, mobility                   | >=5 = high risk          |
| KDIGO AKI | Renal failure stage      | Creatinine rise and/or UOP decrease                          | Stage 1-3                |

## Standard SCC Evidence Synthesis Tables

For any SCC topic, produce:

1. Guideline comparison matrix (SSC vs SCCM vs EAST vs specialty-specific)
2. Protocol summary (agent, dose, route, frequency, duration, monitoring parameters)
3. Landmark trial comparison (study, design, N, population, intervention, primary outcome, result, GRADE)
4. Scoring system reference (components, interpretation, action thresholds)
5. Bundle compliance checklist (element, evidence grade, target compliance)
6. Outcomes table (mortality, ICU LOS, ventilator days, complication rates)
