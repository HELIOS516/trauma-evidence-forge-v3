# Presentation Template — Medium (18-22 Slides)

## 30-35 Minute Medical Education Presentation

> **v3 OPTIMIZED** — Keyword-only body text (3-7 words per bullet, max 50 words/slide).
> Speaker notes carry the full narrative (150-250 words/slide).
> Marine theme (themeId: "marine"). textMode "preserve".
> Every content slide: Assertion title + keyword bullets + KEY STAT + Bottom Line + Sources.

---

## Slide Structure (18-22 slides, 30-35 minutes)

```
Slide  Type              Content Pattern
1      Title             Topic, presenter, date
2      Learning Obj      3-4 objectives (Bloom's verbs)
3      Case              Opening clinical scenario (keyword bullets)
4      Content           Epidemiology — 3 key stats
5      Content           Pathophysiology — key mechanism (single visual slide)
6      Content           Clinical Assessment / Diagnosis
7      Data/Table        Diagnostic Algorithm or Scoring Tool
8      MCQ #1            Case-based question, 4 options
9      MCQ Answer        Reveal + evidence rationale
10     Content           Treatment/Management Tier 1
11     Trial             Key Landmark Trial (PICO table)
12     Content           Treatment/Management Tier 2 / Escalation
13     Data/Table        Evidence Summary Table
14     Guideline         Multi-Society Guideline Comparison
15     Content           Special Populations / Complications
16     MCQ #2            Second case-based question
17     MCQ Answer        Reveal + evidence rationale
18     Case              Case Resolution — return to opening case
19     Take-Home         5 keyword takeaways
20     References        Compact reference list
21-22  Backup            Q&A, additional data
```

## Word Budget

- **Per slide (projected):** ~45 words average
- **Total presentation (projected):** ~900 words max
- **Speaker notes total:** ~3,500 words (150-250 per content slide)
- **MCQ checkpoints:** 2 (every ~8-10 slides)
