## SECTION 9: DISCUSSION QUESTIONS FOR JOURNAL CLUB

### Tier 1: Knowledge (Recall)

1. What was the primary outcome of this study?
2. What was the study design?
3. What were the inclusion/exclusion criteria?

### Tier 2: Comprehension (Understanding)

4. Why did the authors choose this study design?
5. How do the results compare to prior studies on this topic?
6. What are the key limitations of this study?

### Tier 3: Application (Using Knowledge)

7. How would you apply these findings to a patient you saw recently?
8. What changes would you make to your practice based on this study?
9. How would you counsel a patient about this intervention?

### Tier 4: Analysis (Breaking Down)

10. What biases might have affected the results?
11. Are the conclusions justified by the data presented?
12. What alternative explanations exist for the findings?

### Tier 5: Synthesis (Integrating)

13. How does this study fit into the broader literature on this topic?
14. What would be the next logical study to conduct?
15. If you were to design a follow-up study, what would you do differently?
