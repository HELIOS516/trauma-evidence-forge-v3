# Presentation Template — Compact (12-16 Slides)

## 15-20 Minute Medical Education Presentation

> Gamma-optimized markdown. Each slide separated by \n---\n.
> KEYWORD-ONLY body text (3-7 words per bullet, max 45 words/slide).
> Speaker notes carry the full narrative (150-250 words/slide).
> Marine theme (themeId: "marine"). textMode "preserve".

---

## Slide Structure (12-16 slides, 15-20 minutes)

```
Slide  Type              Content Pattern
1      Title             Topic, presenter, date
2      Learning Obj      3 objectives max (Bloom's verbs)
3      Case              Opening clinical scenario (keyword bullets)
4      The Problem       3 key epidemiology stats (big numbers)
5      Evidence Slide 1  Assertion + key figure/data point
6      Key Trial         PICO + primary outcome (one table)
7      The Data          Annotated figure or forest plot
8      MCQ               Case-based question, 4 options
9      MCQ Answer        Reveal + evidence rationale
10     Guidelines        One comparison table (2-3 societies)
11     Case Resolution   Return to case, apply evidence
12     Take-Homes        3-5 keyword takeaways
13     References        Compact reference list
14-16  Backup            Additional trials, special populations, Q&A
```

## Word Budget

- **Per slide (projected):** ~45 words average
- **Total presentation (projected):** ~630 words
- **Speaker notes total:** ~2,100 words (150-250 per content slide)
