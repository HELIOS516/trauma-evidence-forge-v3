## Overview
- **Type:** Trauma (multi-domain review)
- **Duration:** 55-60 minutes
- **Template:** presentation-shelf-review (44 slides)
- **Shelf Exam Weight:** ~20-25% of NBME Surgery Shelf (trauma & critical care domain)
- **Target Audience:** MS3/MS4 on ACS/Trauma rotation — pre-shelf exam review
- **Presenter:** Evan DeCan, MD — UVA Division of Acute Care Surgery
- **Companion Deck:** xABCDE Primary Survey (covers ATLS framework, shock classes, MTP, TXA, FAST)

## Purpose

Rapid-fire shelf exam review covering the **10 trauma domains NOT covered by the xABCDE presentation**. Table-dominant, classification-focused, MCQ-rich. Designed for exam preparation, not grand rounds teaching.

## Learning Objectives
1. **Differentiate** epidural from subdural hematoma by CT pattern, clinical course, and surgical threshold
2. **Classify** spinal cord syndromes by deficit pattern and identify appropriate c-spine clearance pathway
3. **Calculate** burn resuscitation using the Parkland formula and apply burn center transfer criteria
4. **Apply** operative thresholds for hemothorax, pelvic fracture hemorrhage, and penetrating abdominal trauma
5. **Recognize** compartment syndrome, open fracture classification, and indications for emergent fasciotomy
6. **Identify** trauma presentations requiring modified management in pediatric, geriatric, and pregnant patients
