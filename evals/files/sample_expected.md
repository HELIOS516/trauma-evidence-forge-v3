## The VTE Problem in Trauma

**Incidence Without Prophylaxis:**

- General trauma patients: **30-60%** DVT rate <sup>[1]</sup>
- TBI patients: **20-30%** VTE rate <sup>[3]</sup>

**Sources:**
- [1] [Eastern](https://www.east.org/)
- [3] Wu 2023
